Frequently Asked Questions

Where does your price data come from?

We aggregate data from major marketplaces like TCGPlayer, Cardmarket, and eBay sold listings to provide accurate market averages.

How can I tell if my card is graded correctly?

Check the certification number on the respective grading company's website (PSA, Beckett, CGC) and ensure the high-resolution scans match your slab exactly.

Do you buy collections?

We are currently an informational resource and do not buy or sell cards directly.
