Is Your Card Real or Fake?

Compare real vs. fake characteristics side-by-side to ensure your collection is authentic.

Genuine Card

Crisp Text: Fonts are sharp and correct.

Texture: Many modern ultra-rares have physical texture (fingerprint-like ridges).

Light Test: A real card will block most light if a flashlight is shined through it, due to the black graphite layer inside.

Counterfeit Card

Blurry/Wrong Text: Typos, incorrect HP fonts, or washed-out text.

Smooth/Flat: Completely smooth surfaces on cards that should be textured, often with an overly glossy, cheap rainbow reflection.

Light Test: Light shines straight through easily because the card is made of cheap, thin paper.
