How to Play the Pokémon TCG

Follow our step-by-step tutorial to get started.

Step 1: Setting Up

You and your opponent each need a deck of exactly 60 cards. Shuffle your deck and draw 7 cards. Place one Basic Pokémon face down as your Active Pokémon, and up to 5 more on your Bench.

Set aside the top 6 cards of your deck as your Prize cards.

Step 2: Turn Structure

On your turn, you must draw a card. Then, you can do any of the following:

  • Put Basic Pokémon on your Bench.
  • Evolve your Pokémon.
  • Attach 1 Energy card to one of your Pokémon.
  • Play Trainer cards.
  • Retreat your Active Pokémon.
  • Use Abilities.

Example: Evolving a Pokémon or using an Ability like Pyro Dance!

Step 3: Attacking & Winning

Your turn ends when you attack. To attack, your Active Pokémon must have the required Energy attached. Place damage counters on your opponent's Active Pokémon.

If a Pokémon's HP reaches 0, it is Knocked Out. Take 1 Prize card (or more for special Pokémon). You win if you take all 6 Prize cards, knock out all your opponent's Pokémon in play, or if your opponent runs out of cards in their deck!
